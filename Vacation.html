My Dream Vacation: Ladakh

Why I Chose This Place

  • I have always dreamed of visiting Ladakh because of its stunning landscapes, high-altitude mountains, and peaceful monasteries. The region offers a perfect mix of adventure and peace, making it an unforgettable destination for me.

    • Things to Do

    • Ladakh is full of exciting activities, from trekking through the Himalayas and riding bikes on the world's highest motorable roads to visiting Pangong Lake and experiencing Tibetan culture in monasteries. Stargazing in the clear night sky of Ladakh is also a magical experience.
